October 1st, 2024

When the internet originated, its purpose was to help better communication between military and university computers during the Cold War. Since then, the internet has taken on a life of its own, harboring advances such as social media and AI. These additions conjure the question of how social media has affected us as people and how we interact with each other. Research has shown that there are positive effects to social media use. One finding is that social media use that is socially oriented and active can be associated with positive social experiences (Heyman et al., 2021). The notion that social media use can create positive social experiences is interesting considering how often social media is painted as something negative for people to engage in, due to negative mental health effects, cyberbullying, stalking, impersonation, and more.

Despite the negative effects that social media can and has had, I am very grateful for the internet and to have a way to connect with those that I care about even from far distances. Without the internet and social media, I would not be able to readily see my friends and family as easily, nor make new connections with people through common interfaces such as social media platforms. While the internet has evolved in many ways, the main purpose of communication will always be prevalent.
