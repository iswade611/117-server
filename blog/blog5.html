November 21, 2024

Through my time in class, watching the video, and reading the article I selected, I have learned a lot about web design in the past couple of months. While watching “Why is CSS so Weird?”, one idea that really struck me was that CSS will ignore what it doesn’t know and that it has a very intuitive design. I think this goes hand in hand with the article that I read from Smashing Magazine titled “10 Principles of Good Web Design”, where one of the key points was usability over aesthetics and creating a design that people will find easy to navigate and engaging.

While design may be about how things look, I think that UI/UX design really takes into account the user when crafting its product, which is something a bit more unique to the design world. Learning about usability in web design and how intuitive these programs are has helped me to change the way I approach my designs and take more factors into consideration besides just what I think looks nice.
